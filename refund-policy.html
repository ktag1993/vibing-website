Refund Policy

Last updated: October 29, 2025

1) Overview

This Refund Policy applies to Vibing ("Platform") operated by Minepost Technologies Private Limited. We strive to ensure customer satisfaction and outline our refund policy below.

2) Subscription Refunds

  • 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee: If you're not satisfied with your subscription, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase.
  • Auto-Renewal: Subscription renewals are non-refundable. Cancel before your renewal date to avoid charges.
  • Pro-Rated Refunds: We do not offer pro-rated refunds for partial subscription periods.

3) One-Time Purchases

  • Credits/Tokens: Purchases of credits or tokens are non-refundable once used.
  • Unused Credits: Unused credits may be eligible for refund within 14 days of purchase if no usage has occurred.

4) Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues preventing you from using the Platform:

  • Contact support within 7 days of the issue occurring.
  • We will attempt to resolve the technical problem.
  • If the issue cannot be resolved, we may offer a refund or service credit at our discretion.

5) Refund Process

To request a refund:

  • Email us at support@vibing.live with your request.
  • Include your account email, transaction ID, and reason for the refund.
  • We will review and respond within 5-7 business days.
  • Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 10-14 business days.

6) Cancellation Policy

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time through account settings.
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
  • You will retain access to paid features until the end of your billing period.
  • No refunds are provided for the remaining days of your current billing period after cancellation.

7) Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Services already rendered or features already accessed.
  • Subscription renewals (cancel before renewal to avoid charges).
  • Purchases made more than 30 days ago.
  • Accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations.

8) Mobile App Store Purchases

  • iOS (App Store): Purchases made through the Apple App Store are subject to Apple's refund policy. Contact Apple Support for refund requests.
  • Android (Google Play): Purchases made through Google Play are subject to Google's refund policy. Contact Google Play Support for refund requests.

9) Exceptions

We reserve the right to make exceptions to this policy on a case-by-case basis. Exceptional circumstances may qualify for refunds outside the standard policy at our sole discretion.

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. Your continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
