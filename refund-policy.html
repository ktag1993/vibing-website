Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Refund Eligibility

We want you to be completely satisfied with Vibing. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may be eligible for a refund under the following conditions:

  • Refund requests must be made within 7 days of purchase
  • The service must not have been extensively used
  • You must provide a valid reason for the refund request

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable:

  • Downloadable digital content after delivery
  • Services that have been fully delivered
  • Promotional or discounted purchases
  • Subscription renewals (unless cancelled before renewal)

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@vibing.live with:

  • Your order/transaction ID
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for refund request
  • Any relevant screenshots or documentation

4. Refund Processing Time

Once your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • The refund will be credited to your original payment method
  • You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is processed
  • It may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account

5. Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds for:

  • Subscription cancellations mid-billing cycle (prorated)
  • Service issues that affected your experience
  • Cases where full refund criteria are not met but circumstances warrant consideration

6. Subscription Cancellations

For subscription-based services:

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time
  • Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period
  • No refunds for unused time in the current billing cycle (unless otherwise specified)
  • You retain access until the end of the paid period

7. Exceptions

We reserve the right to make exceptions to this policy on a case-by-case basis, particularly in cases of:

  • Technical issues on our end that prevented service delivery
  • Billing errors or unauthorized charges
  • Extenuating circumstances as determined by management

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about our refund policy or need to request a refund, please contact us at:

Email: support@vibing.live

Our support team is available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM (IST) and will respond to your request within 24-48 hours.
